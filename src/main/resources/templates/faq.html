Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is LOST DOCUMENT FINDER?

LOST DOCUMENT FINDER is an online platform designed to help reunite individuals with their lost documents. If someone finds a lost document, they can register it on our website, and the rightful owner can search for and claim it.

2. How does LOST DOCUMENT FINDER work?

  1. A custodian (finder) registers a lost document on the platform by entering its type and serial/document number.
  2. The owner searches for their lost document using the document type and either the serial number or document number.
  3. If the document is found, the owner can claim it after verifying their phone number via OTP.
  4. Once verified, the custodian’s contact details are shared with the owner via website display and SMS.
  5. The owner and custodian arrange to meet in a safe public place for document recovery.

3. What types of documents are supported?

Currently, LOST DOCUMENT FINDER only supports the following documents:

  • National ID
  • Driver’s License
  • Passport

These documents must be issued in Kenya, Uganda, or Tanzania.

4. Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information, including phone numbers, is securely stored and used only for verification and document recovery purposes. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except as required by law.

5. How do I verify my phone number?

To ensure authenticity, both custodians and owners must verify their phone numbers via a One-Time Password (OTP) sent via SMS. This must be completed before registering or claiming a document.

6. Is LOST DOCUMENT FINDER free to use?

Yes, our platform is completely free for both custodians and owners. There are no charges for registering or claiming a lost document.

7. What happens after I claim my document?

Once you claim your document and verify your phone number, you will receive the custodian’s contact details via SMS and website display. You must arrange a meeting in a safe public place to recover your document. LOST DOCUMENT FINDER is not liable for any incidents that occur after contact details have been exchanged.

9. Can I delete my personal data from the platform?

10. What should I do if my document is not found?

If your lost document is not yet registered on the platform, check back later as someone may find and register it. You can also report your missing document to the relevant authorities.
