Process

How It works

If you stumble upon a lost document—whether it’s an ID, passport, or other paperwork—take a moment to act.
Your small effort can make a big difference in helping someone recover something valuable

Step 1

Report the found document on our easy-to-use platform.
Provide details, that the owner can identify and reclaim without hassle.

Step 2

If you’ve lost a document, simply search our website to see if it’s been registered. Thanks to our growing community of helpful finders.

Step 3

Once you confirm a match, we help you connect with the finder to arrange a handover.

Meet in a secure public location and finally reclaim your important document with peace of mind.
