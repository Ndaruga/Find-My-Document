LOST DOCUMENT FINDER

Effective Date: August 17, 2024

1. Introduction

LOST DOCUMENT FINDER ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our platform. By accessing or using LOST DOCUMENT FINDER, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Includes your phone number, required for OTP verification, and any contact details shared when claiming or registering a document.
  • Document Information: Includes document type, serial number, and document number provided by custodians during registration.
  • Usage Data: Data on interactions with the platform, including searches, claims, and registrations.
  • Device and Log Information: We may collect IP addresses, browser types, and access timestamps for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Verification: Phone number verification via OTP to prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Document Matching: Connecting custodians and owners securely.
  • Notifications: Sending SMS notifications to users regarding document claims.
  • Security and Compliance: Ensuring data integrity and compliance with legal frameworks in Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania.
  • Analytics and Improvement: Enhancing user experience and platform performance.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • To Custodians and Owners: Upon successful verification, the custodian's contact details will be shared with the owner via website display and SMS.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or government authorities in Kenya, Uganda, or Tanzania.
  • Fraud Prevention: If necessary to detect or prevent fraudulent activities on the platform.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: Protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access.
  • Access Control: Limiting access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: Monitoring and updating security measures as needed.

6. Data Retention

  • Phone numbers and verification data are stored securely and deleted after a specified period.
  • Document-related data is retained until the document is claimed or deemed inactive.
  • Users may request deletion of their data by contacting us.

7. User Rights and Choices

  • Access: Users have the right to access their personal information stored on our platform.
  • Correction or Deletion: Users may request correction or deletion of inaccurate or unnecessary data.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Users may withdraw consent for data processing, which may limit platform functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or practices. Users should review third-party policies before interacting with external sites.

9. Meeting Arrangements and Liability Disclaimer

  • Once a document is claimed and contact details are shared, it is the responsibility of both parties to arrange a meeting in a safe public place.
  • We do not assume liability for any incidents, disputes, or transactions occurring after contact details have been exchanged.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes via platform updates.
